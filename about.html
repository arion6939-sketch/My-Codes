About Ohrid

Ohrid is located in the south-western part of North Macedonia, on the shore of Lake Ohrid, at an elevation of 695 meters above sea level.

Ohrid has a warm-summer Mediterranean climate (Köppen climate classification: Csb), bordering on an oceanic climate (Cfb) moderated by its elevation.

The coldest month is January with an average temperature of 2.5°C, while the warmest month is August with temperatures reaching 27.7°C.
